Angelika

As part of my internship at Godfrey Dadich Partners, me and a team of five other interns worked on a 7 week long project aimed to refresh a brand of our choice. We chose to work with Angelika Film Center to help elevate their brand, specifically focusing on social media and advertising. I worked alongside the editorial and strategy team to come up with a holistic brand refresh that included new story franchises, new social media marketing strategies, and a new design system. This was a very fruitful collaborative project that I'm very grateful to have been a part of. For a more detailed look at this project, click here to download the presentation.
