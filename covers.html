re-imagined album covers

Listening to music has always been a source of inspiration and creativity for me in terms of work and life in general. I wanted to translate the imaginary world I become a part of when i listen to music, into visuals that fit with the melody of these songs. I challenged myself to create some album covers over the span of roughly a month as a way to practice and also to just have some fun! Hope you enjoy these, and if by any chance you are in a band, and looking for someone to design your album covers, I am open for commisions :)
