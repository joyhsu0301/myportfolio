liminal: an animated short

Liminal is a animated short about a girl alone on a subway late night. She starts to notice a weird black substance appearing everywhere; reality as she knows it begins to warp right in front of her eyes. This animation was drawn frame-by-frame, and entirely on my trackpad. Yes, my trackpad (a big thank you to the 3-finger drag function, couldn't have done it without you). The emptiness of a subway cart after rush hour always felt like a liminal space to me, and thus this became the inspiration for my animation. View the full animation here.
