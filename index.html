Hi, I'm Joy Hsu

I am a multimedia artist whose work includes front-end web design, animation, editorial design, physical and digital collage, and digital illustrations. Through creating self-imposed restraints and rules, I create work that pushes me to reach a solution. My designs predominantly adopt a minimalist style, with an emphasis on using accents or small details to highlight the concept and themes fueling my work. Elements of playfulness and humor are central themes in my work that reflect my attitude and approach towards creating art.
