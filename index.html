Hi, I'm Joy Hsu

I am a multimedia artist whose work includes front-end web design, animation, editorial design, physical and digital collage, and digital illustrations. Through creating self-imposed restraints and rules, I create work that pushes me to think creatively in order to reach a solution through my designs or illustrations. My designs predominantly adopt a minimalist style, with the emphasis on using accents or small details to highlight the concept. Elements of playfulness and humor are the central themes in my work that reflect my attitude and approach towards creating art.
