the time capsule

Going off of the idea of creating a generative website, I decided to create an interactive page where visitors can anonymously leave comments for one another. Using HTML, CSS, Jquery, and Firebase I was able to bring this idea to life.

Initially named the visitor's log, I was inspired to create a digital version of visitor logs that museums or shops sometimes provide for the customers to write in. My intention of wanting people to anonymously interact with one another led me to think of questions or prompts that could connect people regardless of time restraints. I decided on this permanent prompt when I was reminded of the time capsule I made for myself from when I was 12 years old, hence the name of the website.

Click here to go to the website! Feel free to leave a message or two while you're there!
